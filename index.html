About Me

I am a PhD student in distributed computing at UNAM, advised by Armando Castañeda. My research focuses on distributed runtime verification of linearizability, verification interfaces, and asynchronous distributed algorithms.

I earned a B.Sc. in Telecommunications Engineering from the Faculty of Engineering, UNAM (2020), where my thesis was supervised by Francisco García. I later received an M.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from UNAM (2022), advised by Armando Castañeda.
