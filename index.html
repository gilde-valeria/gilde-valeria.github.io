About Me

I am a PhD student at UNAM (México) working on concurrent and distributed computing. My research focuses on distributed runtime verification of linearizability, verification interfaces, and asynchronous distributed algorithms.

Publications

  • Asynchronous wait-free runtime verification and enforcement of linearizability. ACM Symposium on Principles of Distributed Computing (PODC) 2023. [PDF]
  • Towards Efficient Runtime Verified Linearizable Implementations. Runtime Verification (RV) 2024. (Gilde Valeria Rodriguez and Armando Castañeda). [Runner-up Best Paper Award] [PDF] [BibTeX]
  • Asynchronous Fault-Tolerant Language Decidability for Runtime Verification of Distributed Systems. ACM Symposium on Principles of Distributed Computing (PODC) 2025. [PDF]
  • Distributed Runtime Verification of Linearizability Using Non-linearizable Objects. (Journal submission in progress).
